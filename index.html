About Me

I have made an exciting career change to become a Full Stack Developer!

After completing an MSc and PhD in Ecology, I was a technical specialist and manager in the environmental sector, gaining a wide range of experience and transferable skills, such as project management, data analysis, problem-solving, clear written and verbal communication, and working in close-knit teams.

I have enjoyed a successful career so far but I am ready for a new challenge and the opportunity to pursue my passion for coding.

Whilst retraining in my own time as a Full Stack Developer, I transitioned into the tech industry, working for Dronecloud — a Software as a Service flight management system for drones. I performed business analyst, software testing and project management tasks towards a large project to integrate software across three independent companies. This was great experience working within a software development environment, and I was even able to contribute some code of my own to the development team in my own time.

"Tom was awesome in his time here with us at Dronecloud. He brought a huge amount of experience and skills, as well as a willingness and capability to quickly pick up new skills on his own. Tom fit really well into the team and was instrumental in making Project Rise such a success!"Jan Domaradzki, CEO Dronecloud

I am based in Hertfordshire in the UK, and I am available for work.
