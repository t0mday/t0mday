About Me

I am in the middle of an exciting career change to become a Full Stack Developer.

After graduating in Biology at Leeds, I completed an MSc in Ecology at York and then a PhD at Cambridge. I have since worked as a technical specialist and a manager in the environmental sector, where I gained a wide range of experience and skills, such as in project management, data analysis, problem-solving, clear written and verbal communication and working collaboratively with others.

I have been privileged with my career so far but I am now ready for a completely new challenge and the opportunity to pursue my passion for coding. I have always been fascinated by technology, and my first experience with coding was teaching myself C to allow me to create computer models for my PhD.

I have already made big strides towards my new goals. Whilst retraining in my own time as a Full Stack Developer, I transitioned into the technology sector, working for Dronecloud — a Software as a Service flight management system for drones. I have been able to use my existing transferable skills as well as my proactive self-led approach to learning new skills, covering business analyst, software testing and project management roles to assist in delivery of a large project to integrate software across three independent companies. This has been invaluable experience of working within a software development environment, and I was even able to contribute some code of my own to the development team in my own time.

"Tom has been awesome in his time here with us at Dronecloud. He brought a huge amount of experience and skills as well as a capability to quickly pick up new skills on his own. Tom fit really well into the team and was instrumental in making Project Rise such a success!"Jan Domaradzki, CEO Dronecloud

I am based in Hertfordshire in the UK, and I am available for work.
