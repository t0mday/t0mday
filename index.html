About Me

I have made an exciting career change to become a Full Stack Developer!

After completing an MSc and PhD in Ecology, I was a technical specialist and manager in the environmental sector, gaining a wide range of skills, such as project management, building relationships with clients and partners, data analysis, problem-solving and working in close-knit teams.

I am ready for a new challenge and the opportunity to pursue my passion for coding.

Whilst retraining in my own time as a Full Stack Developer, I transitioned into the tech industry, working as a Business Analyst for Dronecloud—a Software as a Service drone platform. My responsibilities included demonstrating software, communicating with stakeholders, test-planning, user acceptance testing and project management towards a large project to integrate software across three independent companies. I even contributed some code to the development team in my own time.

“Tom was awesome in his time here with us at Dronecloud. He brought a huge amount of experience and skills, as well as a willingness and capability to quickly pick up new skills on his own. Tom fit really well into the team and was instrumental in making Project Rise such a success!”Jan Domaradzki, CEO Dronecloud

I live in Hertfordshire in the UK.
